Rexburg Chamber of Commerce Cite Plan

Site Name

Rexburg Chamber of Commerce The site will be a resource for local businesses and residents to find information about the city and the chamber of commerce in Rexburg.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information about the city and the chamber of commerce.

Scenarios

Some Scenarios someone could come to the site with could be: What events will happen in Rexburg? Where can I get the contact information for the chamber's board of directors? What activities are located around Rexburg?

Color Scheme

The dark green in this color scheme will be the nav bar and the light green the hover on the nav bar. I will use the dark green on the footer as well with the copyright text and contact information.

Typography

Typography will be roboto and used on the fonts in this site.
