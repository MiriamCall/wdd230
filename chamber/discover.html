Discover Rexburg

Welcome to Rexburg

Nestled in the heart of eastern Idaho, Rexburg is a charming city brimming with activities and attractions for all ages. Known for its friendly community and picturesque surroundings, Rexburg offers a variety of outdoor adventures, including hiking, fishing, and skiing in the nearby Teton and Yellowstone National Parks. The city is also home to Brigham Young University-Idaho, which hosts numerous cultural events, performances, and educational opportunities. Families can enjoy fun-filled days at local spots like Fat Cats entertainment center, the Rexburg Rapids water park, and the historic Paramount 5 theater. With its vibrant mix of nature, education, and entertainment, Rexburg provides endless opportunities for exploration and enjoyment.
